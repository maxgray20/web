About

I am a medievalist graduate student in the English department and iSchool at the University of Wisconsin-Madison where I study Old English poetry and digital humanities. My writing and research focus on problems of modern and contemporary medievalism, and employ practices of literary translation, documentary poetry and public, open-access digital scholarship. I am also a third-year research assistant on the digital humanities platform project Digital Mappa based on campus at the Center for the History of Print and Digital Culture where I use digital technology to support teaching and publishing in the humanities and higher education.
