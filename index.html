About

Maxwell Gray is an American poet, and library studies and digital humanities graduate student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. His research includes practices of translation, documentary poetry and public digital scholarship. He is also a third-year research assistant on the digital humanities platform project Digital Mappa based on campus at the Center for the History of Print and Digital Culture, where he does project/product management, open-access publication and digital pedagogy. Talk to him about medieval literary studies, academic information technology and contemporary poetry.
