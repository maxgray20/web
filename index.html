About

Maxwell Gray is an American poet and medievalist graduate student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. His writing and research focus on problems of modern and contemporary medievalism, and employ creative practices of translation, documentary poetry and public digital scholarship. He is also a third-year research assistant on the digital humanities platform project Digital Mappa based on campus at the Center for the History of Print and Digital Culture, where he uses digital technology to support teaching and research in the humanities.
