About

I'm a medievalist graduate student in literary and library studies in the English department and the iSchool at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research includes practices of documentary poetry, literary translation and public digital scholarship. I teach college writing and research in the English department. I'm also a research assistant on the digital humanities platform project Digital Mappa based on campus at the Center for the History of Print and Digital Culture, where I do project and product management, open-access publications, and digital pedagogy. Talk to me about poetry, translation, digital humanities, and academic libraries and information technology.
